ANDRÉASSON HISTORY

Andréasson clothing is a new luxorious brand on the market. It was founded late 2020 by its owner, Kasper Emil Andréasson. Before 2020-10-02 Kasper gathered information all around the fashion world, one of his big inspirations was Díor. Kasper traveled cross continents and studied bigger brands in his long visit in Paris, the capital of France.

OUR STYLE

As you might have seen Andréasson clothing is full of gummy bears. It is one of the things in life Kasper admires. The gummybear is supposed to show happines, success, mercy, luck and last but not least fortune. Hence why the clothing is full off goofy gummy bears.

The clothing is eco friendly, comfortable and made out of superior cotton. However, the clothing isn't formal at all, it is supposed to be used for casual comfortable relaxed occasions, a lot of customers chose to wear it almost every casual day.
